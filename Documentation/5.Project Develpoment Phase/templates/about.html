Project Overview

What is OptiCrop AI?

OptiCrop AI is an intelligent crop recommendation system that uses Machine Learning to analyze soil and climate parameters and suggest the most suitable crop for cultivation. It helps farmers make data-driven decisions to maximize yield and minimize resource waste.

  • Analyzes 7 key soil & climate parameters
  • Recommends from 22 crop types
  • Provides confidence scores & crop details
  • Generates downloadable PDF reports
  • Tracks prediction history per user
2200 Records
22 Crops
99%+ Accuracy
Random Forest

Business Problem

Farmers often lack access to scientific soil analysis tools and expert agronomists. Poor crop selection leads to low yields, financial losses, and environmental damage. OptiCrop AI bridges this gap by providing instant, data-driven crop recommendations accessible from any device — empowering farmers with AI at their fingertips.
